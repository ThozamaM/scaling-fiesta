Set nearly 200 years before the events of Game of Thrones, 172 years before the birth of Daenerys Targaryen and about 100 years after the Seven Kingdoms are united by the Targaryen Conquest, the series is based on George R.R. Martin's book Fire and Blood.

The show portrays the events leading up to the beginning of the decline of House Targaryen, a devastating war of succession known as the "Dance of the Dragons".

Old Westerosi houses that are no longer in existence when Game of Thrones takes place, such as: House Velaryon and House Hightower are featured in House of the Dragon and viewers will be able to see the role they played in the fall of the greatest dynasty Westeros had ever seen.
